02 · POSITIONING

Brand Voice & Positioning

Core Insight (from Bruce's gamma draft)

"What if they're not people leaving unlawful lifestyles... but entrepreneurs?"
"Most systems try to fix behavior. That's not the problem. The behavior isn't the issue. It's where it's being applied."

Positioning Summary

This is not a traditional reentry or rehab nonprofit. The voice is:

  • Empowering, not corrective — participants don't need to be "fixed"
  • Direct, not soft — no euphemisms, no pity, no charity language
  • Entrepreneurial, not therapeutic — speaks to ambition, not recovery

Trademarked Terms (use consistently)

Unlawful Entrepreneurship™
Lawful Entrepreneurship™
2nd Chance Entrepreneurs™

Voice Rules

✓ Do say

  • "redirect your skills"
  • "you already have what it takes"
  • "this is for entrepreneurs"

✗ Don't say

  • "rehabilitation"
  • "reform"
  • "broken" / "criminals"
  • "starting over"

Webinar Funnel Flow

Primary Conversion Path

Any Page → "Register for Info Session" CTA → Registration Form → Confirmation Page → Email Sequence → Info Session → Application Form → Program Enrollment

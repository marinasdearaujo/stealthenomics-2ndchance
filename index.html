01 · POSITIONING

Brand Voice & Positioning

"What if they're not people leaving unlawful lifestyles... but entrepreneurs?"
"Most systems try to fix behavior. That's not the problem. The behavior isn't the issue. It's where it's being applied."

The whole site is built around one shift: your audience already knows how to run something. They've taken risks, managed people, moved money under pressure. The system wasn't the problem with what they did. The law was.

That single move changes how the site sounds. Not "we'll help you become a better person" — the goal isn't a different person, it's the same person doing legal work. The voice should be:

  • Direct. No charity language. No "journey of healing." Talk to them like adults who already know how the world works.
  • Honest. Don't oversell. Don't promise. Don't motivate.
  • Specific. Use real verbs. "Ran something," "moved money," "managed people" — not "redirect their drive."

02 · VISUAL DIRECTION

Visual Direction

Color Palette

#023D69 — Primary Blue
#F9870B — Accent Orange

Typography

Headers: Libre Franklin Black/Bold

Body: Tahoma or Aptos

Real people. Real settings. No "second chance" stock imagery — no broken chains, no sunrise photos, no hands reaching out. Photos of actual participants and mentors when possible. The visual tone matches the voice: serious, professional, no theatrics.

Proposed Copy — Section by Section

HERO

Headline

What if they're not people leaving unlawful lifestyles... but entrepreneurs?

Subhead

You already know how to build a business. We help you build one you don't have to hide.

THE PROBLEM
Most systems try to fix behavior. That's not the problem.

Most reentry programs hand you a name tag and tell you to forget what you know.

But you didn't lose your skills when you got caught. You ran something. You managed people. You moved money. You took risks most people wouldn't touch. The system wasn't the problem with what you did — the law was.

The skills don't change. The market does.

THE SOLUTION
We're not asking you to start over.

2nd Chance Entrepreneurs™ is a nonprofit for people who've already proven they can run something — and now want to run something legal.

You get a mentor, a curriculum that's about doing the work (not theory), and a community of people who've been there. The goal isn't a certificate. It's a registered, lawful business with your name on it.

WHAT YOU GET
What you actually get.

Mentorship — A 1-on-1 coach who's built something legal and knows what it actually takes.

Curriculum — Six modules covering the stuff you actually need: legal setup, money, marketing, ops.

Infrastructure — We help with the LLC, the bank account, the website. The basics no one else walks you through.

Community — A room full of people walking the same path.

FOUNDER
The person behind this.

Bruce "Captain" Kirk has spent years working with founders, building businesses, and watching the system fail people who had every quality of a great entrepreneur — except a legal way to use it.

He started 2nd Chance Entrepreneurs™ because he was tired of waiting for someone else to.

HOW IT WORKS
Four steps. No mystery.
  1. Info Session — A free, no-pressure walk-through of how the program works. Nothing to sign.
  2. Apply — A short application. We want to know what you've done and where you're trying to go.
  3. Match + Start — We pair you with a mentor and the curriculum that fits where you are.
  4. Launch — A registered business. Customers. Money in a real bank account.
WHO IT'S FOR
This isn't a course. It isn't easy. And it isn't for everyone.

What you get: a mentor who's built something legal, a curriculum that skips the fluff, weekly meetings that actually move things forward, and a community that gets where you're coming from.

You bring the work. We bring everything else.

OUR COMMITMENT
What we promise — and what we don't.

We won't promise you success. Anyone who promises that is selling something.

Here's what we will promise: we show up. We work next to you, not at you. Anything we know, we'll teach. And we don't quit on you when it gets hard.

CLOSE
You're not starting over.

You already know how to do this. You just need to do it on the right side of the line.

Come to the info session. See if it's a fit. No commitment.

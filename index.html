Section 07

Technical Requirements & Development Phases

What the dev team needs to build, and in what order.

CMS

WordPress or equivalent. Client must be able to post updates independently.

Donations

Stripe or PayPal with recurring support. Fiscal sponsor compliant.

Email Capture

Mailchimp, HubSpot, or ConvertKit for volunteers, donors, and participants.

Analytics

Google Analytics 4. Nonprofits qualify for free GA 360.

Google Ad Grant

$10,000/month in free Google Ads once 501(c)(3) is approved.

Performance

Mobile-first. Load under 2.5s. SSL/HTTPS. WCAG 2.2 accessibility.

Development Phases

P0 — Launch

Get Online (4 pages)

  • Homepage (mission, programs, impact, CTAs)
  • About page (founder story)
  • Donate page (fiscal sponsor compliant)
  • Contact page (includes volunteer/mentor form)
  • Email capture on all pages
P1 — Expand

Add Depth

  • Events page (workshops, community events)
  • Google Business Profile
  • Social media profiles
  • Newsletter setup
P2 — Grow

Scale Reach

  • Blog (SEO content engine)
  • Testimonials & success stories
  • Google Ad Grant application
  • Partner logos, board of directors page

Ongoing

Content updates, impact reports, event promotion, blog publishing, SEO optimization
